Time Management for Software Developers

Your playbook for deep work, smart prioritization, and sustainable growth.

This book helps developers regain control of their time — not by working longer hours, but by working with more intention. Learn how to protect your focus, prioritize high-value tasks, and build a sustainable career beyond the sprint board.
